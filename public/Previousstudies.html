Get to know the brain at school

I worked on the project Découvrir le Cerveau et le Contrôle Cognitif au Cycle 1 (D4C). This projects aims to determined wether a neuroeducational program helping develop metacognition has an impact on self regulation in 5-6 years old children. In this project, I worked on the effect of attentionnal skills on executive functions in 5 years old children.
